# KAN-28: CHK Facilities Collaboration – KAVC

## Description

We see KAVC as the premier fully integrated sports, wellness, and recreation partner in the Philippines. We believe it would be mutually beneficial to explore potential avenues for collaboration with the College of Human Kinetics facilities.Initial areas of engagement may include:- Internship Programs

 – Providing students with hands-on experience in sports management, event operations, wellness programs, athlete services, marketing, and recreation initiatives through structured internship opportunities with KAVC.


- Volunteers for Athletic Events

 – Engaging CHK students as event volunteers for local and international athletic events organized by KAVC, allowing them to gain practical exposure in tournament operations, athlete coordination, logistics, and sports event management.
