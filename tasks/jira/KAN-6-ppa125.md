# KAN-6: PPA125

## Description

PPA 125 Philippines

 is a Professional Pickleball Association (PPA) tournament series held across the Philippines, bringing together top local and international pickleball athletes in professionally organized competitions. The series will feature events in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao particularly in Manila, Cebu, and Davao as part of the mission to grow the sport nationwide and make competitive pickleball more accessible to Filipino players.By staging qualifying-level tournaments across key regions, PPA 125 Philippines aims to give more Filipinos the opportunity to join, compete, and prepare for the upcoming PPA Open Philippines in January 2027, while further positioning the country as a major pickleball hub in Asia.
