# Play by Point

## Overview
Play by Point is a facility management platform for racquet sports (pickleball, tennis, padel). It provides mobile check-in, access control, and operational tools through a club app.

## Core Offerings
- **Mobile Check-in & Access**: App-based court access and member check-in
- **Facility Management**: Booking, scheduling, court availability
- **Member Management**: Membership tracking and engagement
- **Club Operations**: Tools to manage day-to-day facility operations

## Service Types Supported
- Pickleball
- Tennis  
- Padel

## Competitive Position
Play by Point focuses on **facility management and core operations**—the booking system, court scheduling, and member check-in layer. This is foundational infrastructure that many facilities need.

## Differentiation from Helios
**Play by Point:** Facility operations, booking, scheduling, member management  
**Helios (PodPlay):** Facility management for structured play (tournaments, leagues, leaderboards)

These are **complementary, not competing** services. A facility can use Play by Point for core operations and Helios for organized competitive play management.

- Integration opportunity: Helios can integrate with Play by Point's event workflow
