## Upcharge Split

Bookings above the $50 minimum floor are subject to a revenue share:

- **70%** — Kosmas (local partner / venue operator)
- **30%** — Pod Play

Split applies to the upcharge amount above the $50 minimum, not the full booking value.

## Withholding Tax

- Philippines withholding tax: **17%**
- Must be factored into net revenue projections and partner remittance calculations.

## Payment Processing

- **Magpie** is the designated payment processor for all Philippines venues.
- Supports GCash and credit card payments.
- Stripe does not operate in the Philippines — do not use for PH venues.
- Revenue split negotiation with Magpie is ongoing — see `data/vendors/magpie.md`.
