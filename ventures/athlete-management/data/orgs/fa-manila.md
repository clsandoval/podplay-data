## Open due-diligence items (per [[richard-bachmann]])
Before committing capital, need clarity on:
- **What we acquire** beyond the player/member base: league participation rights, brand equity, operational infrastructure, coaching system, existing sponsor relationships
- **Corporate documents** (SEC-registered, so request): latest GIS, latest AFS, cap table / ownership structure, banking information, existing liabilities or obligations
- **Operations & personnel:** which coaching staff, the current Spanish Technical Director, and admin personnel are retained vs. contractual? Interview admin team and selected coaches.
- **League transition:** if the academy rebrands to Dragonforce FA Manila mid-season → no league restrictions per JC (only academy name changes). Confirmed.
- **Player transfers:** under new youth league rules, mid-season transfers disqualify the player for that season. → **World Camp timing: end-of-season (Feb–Mar 2027 if league ends May 2027)** to minimize player wait periods.
- **Membership retention risk** during a 2–3 year build period — biggest business risk; need retention strategy + risk-sharing terms.
