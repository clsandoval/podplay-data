## Company Summary
Reset Pickleball is a specialized equipment company in the pickleball space focused on paddle maintenance and performance optimization. They manufacture Paddle Reset™, a proprietary cleaning solution specifically formulated for pickleball paddles.

## Product Overview
**Paddle Reset™** is a science-based paddle cleaning solution that addresses a critical pain point for dedicated players—paddle performance degradation from ball residue and dust buildup.

### Product Details
- **Purpose:** Restore paddle performance by removing gummy ball residues, caked-on dust, and polymer buildup
- **Formulation:** Proprietary emulsifiers strip through plasticizers, elastomers, and polymer scuffs
- **Delivery:** Spray bottle with non-toxic cleaning solution
- **Usage:** Apply to paddle surface after each match, wipe with microfiber towel
- **Benefits:** Restores sharp factory surface textures, improving control, spin, and reducing ball slip
- **Distribution:** Available through PickleballCentral.com and their own e-commerce site (resetpickleball.shop)
- **Marketing Tagline:** "PLAY HARD, PLAY CLEAN"

### Target Customer
Serious/dedicated pickleball players who understand that paddle surface condition directly impacts performance—particularly high-frequency players who accumulate significant residue buildup.

## Industry Context
The pickleball industry is experiencing explosive growth with strong tailwinds:
- Pickleball grew 223.5% over three years in the US (as of 2024)
- 13.6 million people played pickleball in 2023
- Outdoor pickleball courts in major US cities increased 650% over the last seven years
- Global pickleball market valued at $2.2B in 2024, projected to reach $9.1B by 2034

Reset Pickleball is positioned to capture margin in the consumables/accessories segment of this rapidly expanding market.

## Market Positioning
Reset Pickleball operates in the equipment/accessories vertical rather than facility operations. Their focus on paddle maintenance aligns with the growing sophistication and professionalization of pickleball players seeking performance optimization.
