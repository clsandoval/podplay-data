## Overview

A cross-facility credit system that allows users to load funds into a PodPlay wallet and spend across any participating venue. Credits are portable across the PodPlay network.

## Philippines Advantage

Electronic wallet refunds in the Philippines are **one-way** — once funds are loaded, they stay within the system as credits. This structurally improves cash flow and reduces refund liability compared to card-based payments.

## Payment Partnership

- Built on **Magpie** for GCash + credit card funding in the Philippines.
- See `data/vendors/magpie.md` for partnership and revenue split status.

## Merchant Products

Wallet credits can be applied toward:
- Court bookings
- Coaching sessions
- Merchandise
- Food & beverage (F&B)

## Expansion

- Singapore and Malaysia are potential expansion markets.
- Local partners would be required for regulatory compliance and payment integration in each market.
